About CareOne

A Purpose-Built
Healthcare Company

For over a decade, CareOne Services Ltd has been the trusted partner for hospitals, clinics, and healthcare institutions seeking excellence in equipment, training, and management.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, reliable, and affordable healthcare solutions that empower medical institutions to deliver exceptional patient care and build sustainable operational capacity.

Our Vision

To be Africa's most trusted healthcare services company — recognized for transforming healthcare outcomes through superior technology, expertise, and a relentless commitment to quality.

Our Values

Integrity, excellence, innovation, and people-first thinking guide every project, partnership, and service we deliver to our clients across the region.

Our Story

More Than a Decade of Impact

CareOne Services Ltd was founded with a simple conviction: that every healthcare institution, regardless of size or location, deserves access to world-class equipment, expertise, and support.

From our headquarters in Enugu, Nigeria, we have grown into a multidisciplinary healthcare services company serving clients across Nigeria and beyond. Our team of doctors, engineers, consultants, and trainers brings together a rare combination of clinical insight and business acumen.

We are registered, certified, and fully compliant with Nigerian healthcare regulatory requirements, and maintain active partnerships with leading medical equipment manufacturers in Europe, Asia, and the Americas.
